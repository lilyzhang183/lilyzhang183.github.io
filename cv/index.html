  • Sep 2023 -- Dec 2023
    Guarding Orthogonal Art Galleries with Sliding Cameras
    • MLSC on orthogonal polygons: reduced guarding to a minimum-weight vertex cover on a bipartite graph via the \(r\)-cut partition; for \emph{monotone} polygons the instance is \emph{convex} bipartite, giving an \(O(n\log^2 n)\) cover routine.
    • For FAT/MIN-AREA grid \(n\)-gons on a uniform grid, established equal-length segments, yielding an \emph{unweighted} convex bipartite instance with an \(O(n)\) cover step.
  • Sep 2020 -- Dec 2020
    Cerebral Cortex — Geometric & Topological Models
    • Graph-based topology correction for 3D cortical volumes: slice-wise foreground/background connectivity, max-spanning-tree pruning of weak links, and minimal voxel edits iterated across axes.
    • Validated via Marching Cubes meshes and Euler characteristic checks ($\chi=2$) on toy volumes and white-matter MRI masks.

Pure Mathematics Projects

  • Sep 2025 -- Present
    Topics in Factorial Schur Functions
    • Survey of factorial Schur functions: definitions (ratio-of-alternants, tableaux), identities (Cauchy/dual Cauchy, Pieri-Giambelli), worked examples; annotated notes and computations (course project).
    • Variations and links: six-vertex/Yang-Baxter and Tokuyama-type formulas; K-theoretic analogues—factorial $P/Q$ for Schubert classes in $T$-equivariant $K$-theory of isotropic Grassmannians, with Pfaffian and set-valued tableau formulas and the GKM/divided-difference framework.
  • Sep 2021 -- Dec 2021
    Understanding Burnside's Theorem
    • Expository, character-theoretic proof of Burnside’s $p^a q^b$ theorem via Sylow theory, the class equation, column orthogonality, and algebraic-integer methods.
    • Proved supporting lemmas (e.g., $|\chi(g)|\le \chi(1)$ and a conjugacy-class size criterion) and deduced solvability of groups of order $p^a q^b$.
  • Jan 2020 -- May 2020
    Baire Category Theorem and Its Applications
    • Exposition of the Baire Category Theorem (equivalent formulations) with applications to the Uniform Boundedness Principle and the Closed Graph Theorem.
    • Worked examples: a generic but measure-zero set; Grothendieck’s theorem on closed subspaces of $L^p$ contained in $L^\infty$.

Applied Math / Statistics Projects

  • Sep 2022 -- Dec 2022

  • Jan 2022 -- May 2022
    Understanding Housing Bubble in Vancouver, BC — A Time Series Analysis
    • Log-HPI (2005--2021): ADF; ACF/PACF-guided SARIMA $(2,1,0)\times(2,0,0)_{12}$ chosen by AIC/BIC; Ljung--Box diagnostics; 12-month forecasts.
    • VAR(1) on $\Delta$HPI, GDP, 10-year yield, CAD/USD, employment, earnings, CPI, crime + Bill~28 dummy; Granger tests, Cholesky IRFs, FEVD; results: HPI variance mostly self-driven; GDP predictive (95\%); employment marginal (90\%); CPI/yield shocks short-run; policy coefficient negative/significant.
  • Jan 2022 -- May 2022
    Cozy Home
    • ODE indoor-climate model with Fourier heat loss and window air exchange; device effects via sparse $M_1,M_2$ on binary $Y$; quadratic discomfort with PD $S_i$; derived $c_{\mathrm{opt}}=(\sum_i S_i)^{-1}\sum_i S_i c_{p,i}$ and $S_{\mathrm{eff}}=\sum_i S_i$.
    • Time-weighted least squares (daily/weekly/annual kernels) to estimate $c_{\mathrm{opt}}(t)$, $S_{\mathrm{eff}}(t)$, and conductivity $\sigma$; implemented a thresholded control rule for device switching.
